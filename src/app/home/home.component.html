Welcome to My Learning Strategy


Flash Cards
  • Create new subjects for your own flash cards.
  • With each question you can setup multiple answers.
  • To help with the memorization you can embed pictures to help with Visualization.
  • The use of Acronyms is often helpful so we have a field just for that.
Memorization Techniques
  • You can setup how the flash cards will shuffle or be selected to present to you.
  • Define a random number of cards to be selected with a minim of 4 to a maximum of 10 cards.
  • Use categories to you can chunk the data into manageable pieces.

Assessments
  • Randomly select from questions and responses.
  • Randomly sort the flash cards by questions, responses
  • Select responses randomly, or by correct or incorrect answers or both.
